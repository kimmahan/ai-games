Dunning-Kruger Effect: Tech Skill Journey

This graph illustrates the Dunning-Kruger effect in the context of developing tech skills:

  • Mt. Stupid: Early confidence after basic success (e.g., building a simple website)
  • Trough of Despair: Realization of complexity at Level 2-3, working with APIs and data stores
  • Slope of Enlightenment: Gradual, realistic confidence growth with experience
